# Project Description: 
  This website is all about the Dental Clinic of Dr. Margie P. Lipon. Lipon Dental clinic is a patient friendly dental clinic located in Cogeo, Antipolo City that is aiming to provide a quality oral care and a comfortable environment for every patient. This clinic is led by Dr. Margie P. Lipon and her clinic offers a variety of services that focuses on mainting a healthy, strong teeth and enhances the patients' confidence to smile. The clinic is known for its gentle care and its approachable service. This clinic is a good and trusted choice for individuals and families who wants to have a great care for their oral health.
