The Dentist's Triple Role

Warm, Professional Service

At Lipon Dental Clinic, we believe dentistry is about more than just teeth—it's about people. Led by Dr. Margie Payno-Lipon, our team is committed to making every visit comfortable.

Our philosophy, often cited in our clinic, highlights that a dentist must be a dedicated doctor, an engineer to ensure function, and an artist to craft a beautiful smile.
